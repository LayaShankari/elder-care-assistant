# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and
welcoming experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different levels of experience and different viewpoints.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Insults, personal attacks, or abusive comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any behavior that makes the project unsafe or unwelcoming

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit
participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project
maintainers privately with details of the incident.
