## Getting Started

### Accessing the Platform

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. **Go to**: `http://localhost:3000` (or provided URL)
3. **You'll see the login page**

### First-Time Setup

If you're a **new user**, you'll need to create an account:

1. **Click "Sign Up" or "Register"**
2. **Enter your information**:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number (for notifications)
   - Password (8+ characters, mix of letters and numbers)

3. **Create your account** and log in

4. **Complete your profile**:
   - Add emergency contacts
   - List current medications
   - Provide health information

## Account Management

### Logging In

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Log In"
4. If enabled: complete the 2-factor authentication (check your email or phone)

**Tip**: Use "Remember me" on trusted devices only.

### Resetting Your Password

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Updating Your Profile

1. **Tap your name/photo** in the top right
2. **Select "Settings" or "Profile"**
3. **Make changes**:
   - Display name
   - Phone number
   - Emergency contact information
   - Health conditions
   - Current medications

4. **Save changes**

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice
5. Click **"Update"**

## Chat with AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is available 24/7 to chat, answer questions, and provide support.

### Starting a Chat

1. **Look for the "Chat" icon** in the main menu
2. **Type your message** in the message box at the bottom
3. **Press Enter or click "Send"**
4. **Wait for the AI's response**

### Chat Topics You Can Discuss

✓ **Health questions** (general wellness advice)
✓ **Medication side effects**
✓ **Lifestyle and nutrition tips**
✓ **Fitness and exercise ideas**
✓ **Mental health and emotional support**
✓ **Daily living questions**
✓ **General information requests**

### Chat Tips

- **Be specific**: "I have a headache" → "I have a headache on the left side since morning"
- **Mention medications**: Helps the AI give relevant advice
- **Ask follow-up questions**: You can ask for clarification
- **Take notes**: Write down important advice

### Important Notes

⚠️ **The AI is not a doctor**:
- For emergencies, call 911 immediately
- For serious health concerns, contact your doctor
- The AI provides general wellness information only
- Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions

### Viewing Chat History

1. Go to **Chat** section
2. **Previous conversations** appear in the left sidebar
3. **Click any conversation** to view it again

### Clearing Chat History

1. Go to **Chat Settings**
2. Click **"Clear History"**
3. Choose time period (Last hour, Today, All)
4. Confirm deletion

## Health Monitoring

Track your daily health with quick check-ins.

### Daily Health Check-In

Every day, you can record:
- **How you're feeling** (scale 1-10)
- **Energy level**
- **Sleep quality**
- **Any symptoms or concerns**
- **Mood/emotions**

**To submit a check-in**:

1. Look for **"Health" or "Check-In"** in the menu
2. Click **"Daily Check-In"** or **"New Check-In"**
3. **Answer the questions**
4. **Optionally add notes**: "Felt a bit tired today" or "Great day, very energetic"
5. **Submit**

### Tracking Vital Signs

If you have home monitoring equipment:

1. Go to **Health** → **Vital Signs**
2. **Add a new reading**:
   - Blood pressure
   - Heart rate
   - Temperature
   - Weight

3. **Enter the values**
4. **Add date and time** (auto-filled with today's date)
5. **Save**

### Viewing Health History

1. Go to **Health** → **History**
2. **Select date range**: Last week, Last month, Last 3 months
3. **View your trends**:
   - Charts showing mood over time
   - Energy level patterns
   - Weight trends (if tracked)
   - Symptom notes

### Health Insights

The system may show you:
- **Trends**: "Your energy is usually higher on weekends"
- **Patterns**: "You often report headaches after certain activities"
- **Suggestions**: "Based on your patterns, we recommend..."

## Medication Reminders

Stay on top of your medications with reminders.

### Setting Up Reminders

1. Go to **Reminders** → **New Reminder**
2. **Select reminder type**: Medication
3. **Choose medication**:
   - Select from your medication list
   - Or add new medication (name, dosage, frequency)

4. **Set reminder details**:
   - **Frequency**: Daily, Twice daily, Weekly, Custom
   - **Time**: What time should you be reminded?
   - **Number of doses**: How many times per day?

5. **Notification method** (choose one or more):
   - In-app notification
   - Email reminder
   - SMS text message
   - Phone call

6. **Save reminder**

### Example Setup

**Medication**: Blood pressure pill
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Time**: 8:00 AM
- **Notification**: App + Email

### Viewing Your Reminders

1. Go to **Reminders** section
2. See all upcoming reminders
3. **Green check** = Already taken
4. **Yellow clock** = Upcoming
5. **Red bell** = Overdue

### Confirming You Took Your Medication

When you receive a reminder:

1. **See the notification**
2. Click **"I took it"** or **"Confirm"**
3. Optionally add notes: "With food" or "Caused slight dizziness"

**Or manually confirm**:
1. Go to Reminders section
2. Find the reminder
3. Click the checkbox or **"Mark as taken"**

### Editing Reminders

1. Go to **Reminders**
2. **Click on a reminder**
3. **Click "Edit"**
4. **Make changes**
5. **Save**

### Disabling or Deleting Reminders

1. Go to **Reminders**
2. **Find the reminder**
3. **Click menu (three dots)**
4. **Select "Disable" or "Delete"**
5. **Confirm**

### Medication History

Track which medications you've taken:

1. Go to **Reminders** → **History**
2. **View adherence** (what percentage you took)
3. **See past records** for specific dates

## Family Connectivity

Stay connected with family members.

### Adding Family Members

1. Go to **Family** → **Add Member**
2. **Enter family member's email address**
3. **Select their relationship**: Daughter, Son, Spouse, Friend, Caregiver, etc.
4. **Choose what they can see**:
   - ☑ Health status
   - ☑ Medication reminders
   - ☑ Chat history (optional)
   - ☑ Emergency contacts

5. **Click "Invite"**
6. **They'll receive an email** to accept the invitation

### Managing Family Members

1. Go to **Family** → **Members**
2. **View all connected family members**
3. **Click on a member** to:
   - Change what they can see
   - Remove them
   - View shared information

### Sending Updates to Family

1. Go to **Family** → **Send Update**
2. **Write a message**: "I had a great day, feeling good!"
3. **Attach optional info**:
   - Today's health check-in
   - Recent photos
   - Notes

4. **Click "Send"**
5. **Family members receive notification**

### Viewing Family Messages

1. Go to **Family** → **Messages**
2. **See all messages** from family members
3. **Reply to messages**
4. **Share your thoughts or updates**

### Family Can See

When you share with family, they can view:
- Your health check-ins (mood, energy, sleep)
- Medication adherence (if taking meds on time)
- Your general status updates
- Emergency contact information

## Emergency Features

Access emergency help quickly when needed.

### Emergency Alert (SOS Button)

**For life-threatening emergencies**:

1. **Tap the red "SOS" or "Emergency" button** (usually bottom-right or top-left)
2. **Confirm the alert** (to prevent accidental triggers)
3. **System will**:
   - Alert your emergency contacts
   - Send your location (if available)
   - Display emergency contact numbers

⚠️ **For actual medical emergencies, also call 911 directly!**

### Emergency Contacts

**Setting up emergency contacts**:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Emergency Contacts**
2. Click **"Add Contact"**
3. Enter:
   - Full name
   - Relationship
   - Phone number
   - Email address (optional)

4. **Mark as "Primary"** for your main contact
5. **Save**

### Quick Access to Emergency Info

When you press the SOS button, family members will see:
- Your name and age
- Your location (if enabled)
- Your emergency contacts
- Any medical alert information
- Current medications

### Medical Alert Information

Set up medical alerts that display during emergencies:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Medical Information**
2. Add:
   - Allergies
   - Blood type
   - Chronic conditions
   - Current medications
   - Doctor contact information

3. **Save** (this displays in emergencies)

## Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"

**Solutions**:
1. Check that Caps Lock is OFF
2. Make sure you're using the correct email
3. Try "Forgot Password" if you've forgotten your password
4. Clear browser cache and cookies (Search: "clear cache" in your browser)
5. Try a different browser

### "I'm not getting reminders"

**Check**:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Ensure notifications are enabled for your device
3. Check your email spam folder
4. Verify reminder time is correct
5. Check notification method (app/email/SMS)

### "The chat isn't responding"

**Try**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Wait a few seconds and try again
5. Sign out and sign back in

### "I don't see my medication reminder"

**Check**:
1. Go to **Reminders** and verify it exists
2. Check if reminder is "enabled" (not disabled)
3. Verify the time is correct
4. Check notification settings

### "Messages from family aren't arriving"

**Check**:
1. Ask family member if they sent the message
2. Check your internet connection
3. Look in all tabs/folders of the app
4. Check email spam folder (if sent via email)
5. Try refreshing the page

### "I accidentally deleted something important"

**Options**:
1. Check the **Trash** or **Recycle Bin** (if available)
2. Contact support - they may be able to recover recent data
3. Use your browser's **Undo** (Ctrl+Z or Cmd+Z)

### "The app is slow or freezing"

**Try**:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Restart your browser
3. Clear cache and cookies
4. Use a different browser
5. Restart your computer/device

### Performance Issues

**Browser troubleshooting**:
- Use a newer browser version (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Close unnecessary tabs
- Restart your computer
- Check internet connection speed
- Try on a different device

## Tips for Success

### Getting the Most from Elder Care Assistant

✅ **Daily Check-Ins**
- Spend 2 minutes each morning on your health check-in
- Be honest about how you feel
- Track patterns over time

✅ **Chat Usage**
- Ask questions about your health
- Use it for daily emotional support
- Share concerns with the AI

✅ **Medication Management**
- Set reminders for the exact time you take meds
- Confirm each medication immediately after taking it
- Keep notes on how you feel after taking meds

✅ **Family Sharing**
- Send weekly updates to family
- Share health milestones
- Let them know you're doing well

✅ **Emergency Preparedness**
- Keep emergency contacts up-to-date
- Update medical information regularly
- Ensure family knows how to find emergency info

### Staying Safe Online

🔐 **Security Tips**:
- **Use strong passwords**: Mix letters, numbers, symbols
- **Never share your password** with anyone (even family)
- **Log out** on shared devices
- **Update your password** every 3 months
- **Enable 2-factor authentication** if available
- **Don't click suspicious links** in emails

### Getting Help

**If you need assistance**:
1. **Check this manual** - many answers here!
2. **Contact support team** - look for "Help" or "Contact Us"
3. **Ask a family member** to help you navigate
4. **Call technical support** (phone number on website)

### Accessibility Features

If you have difficulty seeing or using the app:
- **Increase text size**: Browser settings (Ctrl++ or Cmd++)
- **High contrast mode**: Often in browser or system settings
- **Screen reader**: Your device can read text aloud
- **Large mouse cursor**: System accessibility settings
- **Ask for help**: Family members can help navigate
